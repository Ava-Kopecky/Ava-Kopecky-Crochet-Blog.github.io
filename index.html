Welcome to Ava's Crochet Blog!

A little about me:

My dad taught me how to crochet when I was very young. He used to make hats and scarfs for my stuffed animals all the time and I wanted to learn to do it too! Though crochet proved to be a bit too hard for 5 year old me. However, it wasn't until 2019 that I started to really pursue crochet. I found an adorable pattern online and spent hours working on it, teaching myself how to crochet by watching YouTube tutorials and reading other crochet blogs. Ever since, I've been obsessed!

On the right is a picture of me when I was about 11 or 12. I loved this YouTuber called LDShadowLady and decided to make her a crochet version of her cat, Buddy! There was a convention happening called Vidcon where a bunch of famous YouTubers would get together and host panels and meet and greets, and LDShadowLady happened to be going. I decided to buy tickets and go with my friend, and we were lucky enough to be able to meet LDShadowLady and her boyfriend! I was able to give her the crochet cat and even made it into their blog because of it! Since then, I began to crochet all the time!

Over the years I have crocheted countless objects. From blankets to hats to the even more complicated amigurumi (crocheted stuffed animals) to keychains and much more! Crochet has always been an important way that I relax, and it's so much fun to see your creations come to life!

This past January I received an amazing opportunity to sell my crochet creations at a craft fair on Ring Road! I was a little worried about how to price my pieces, as even small projects like keychains or headbands take a very long time, however I was able to sell a ton and gained amazing experience for future farmer's markets and craft fairs! It was also a lot of fun to see everyone else's crafts there. Some were also crochet, but others included stained glass decorations, jewelry, and more!
